The problem

Everything important still waits on a person being free.

Inside the organisation, a new starter asks their manager, a technician asks the senior engineer, a branch asks head office — the same questions, answered from memory by the same few people. Outside it, an enquiry lands at 9pm and waits until someone is back at their desk.

Knowledge held in people doesn’t scale — it queues. And demand that waits, cools. Both are capacity you’re paying for and losing.

How Mikora works

One layer between everything you know and everyone who needs it.

Mikora sits across the organisation — connected to what you know, present in every channel where your people and your customers reach you.

01 — CONNECT

Connect your knowledge and your channels

Your procedures, policies, training and expert knowledge become one structured organisational memory — reviewed, approved and owned by you. Your website, WhatsApp and Meta and Google lead forms connect into the same layer.

02 — ANSWER & CAPTURE

Answer and capture in real time

Employees and customers ask in the channels they already use, and Mikora answers in seconds from your approved knowledge. Every new enquiry is captured, scored and qualified the moment it arrives — nothing waits for someone to be free.

03 — ROUTE, SYNC & ESCALATE

Route, sync and escalate

Hot leads alert the right person instantly and sync to Zoho or HubSpot. High-risk questions escalate to a specialist with full context. And every interaction — answered, captured or escalated — feeds your operational picture.

Questions

Asked before you ask.

Is Mikora a chatbot?

Chat is one way people reach Mikora — your team inside WhatsApp or internal messaging, your customers on your website. The product is what sits underneath: structured organisational memory, lead capture and qualification, escalation logic that routes risk and revenue to humans, and an intelligence layer that shows leaders how knowledge and demand move through the business. The conversation is the access point, not the system.

Where do Mikora’s answers come from?

From your organisation — your procedures, policies, training material and expert input, reviewed and approved before they are used. Mikora doesn’t answer from the open internet, and your knowledge isn’t shared beyond your organisation.

Can Mikora handle customer enquiries and our CRM, not just internal questions?

Yes. The same layer answers customers on your website and WhatsApp, captures and qualifies leads from Meta and Google, and syncs with Zoho, HubSpot and others — routing hot leads to the right person automatically. Internally, it answers your own people from your approved knowledge. One operational layer across both.

What happens when a question is high-risk or uncertain?

It is escalated immediately to the person your organisation has designated, with the full context of the conversation attached — rather than answered automatically. Escalation paths are configured around your structure and your standards.

How is Mikora set up?

Each deployment is configured around your organisation: your knowledge sources, communication standards, escalation pathways and reporting requirements. You don’t adapt to Mikora — it is shaped to how you already operate.

Which industries does Mikora serve?

Any organisation that runs on expertise and consistency — from healthcare and manufacturing to franchise networks, field operations and professional services. The platform is industry-agnostic; the configuration is specific to you.

What does Mikora replace?

Usually nothing. It sits alongside your existing systems as a layer between your people and what your organisation knows — reducing the load on your experts, not the tools you’ve already invested in.
